ERAU Cloud Chasers
Created and founded Embry-Riddle's first and only, cloud watching club. The ERAU Cloud Chasers' mission is to foster a deeper appreciation and understanding of atmospheric phenomena, particularly cloud formations. We aim to engage students in the observation, identification, and study of clouds, while promoting awareness of their role in weather patterns and environmental science. Through educational activities and discussions, we strive to cultivate a community of weather enthusiasts and future meteorologists who are passionate about the skies.
